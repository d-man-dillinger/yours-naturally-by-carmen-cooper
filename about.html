About Me

I'm an aspiring naturopath that has been working within the natural health industry for the past 3 years. By day I split my time between working as a colon hydrotherapist at Alkaline in Sydney and studying Naturopathy at Nature Care College in St Leonards.

By night I blog.

I want to inspire people through my writing and experiences to make educated choices about nutrition and natural health so that they can live life at their optimum. I'm learning so much day to day it only seems fair to share.
